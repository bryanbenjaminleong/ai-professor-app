# Origin Labs — Brand Identity Guide

## Logo Concept

### Symbol: The Origin Point

The logo is built around the concept of an **origin point** — the starting place from which all workflows begin.

**Visual elements:**
- A clean circle representing the "origin" — the source, the beginning
- Two intersecting arcs within, suggesting:
  - Connection between human and AI
  - The two products (Crosspath + Mimphes) working in harmony
  - Infrastructure that enables flow
- Negative space creates a subtle "O" for Origin

**Typography:**
- Wordmark: "Origin Labs" in Inter Bold
- Clean, geometric, confident
- "Origin" in primary color, "Labs" in dark charcoal
- Even letter-spacing for balance

**Symbolism:**
- Circle = wholeness, foundation, origin
- Intersecting arcs = collaboration, synthesis, flow
- Clean lines = technical precision, infrastructure
- Open form = accessible, enabling, not closed off

## Design Inspiration

### Why Blue?

Blue is the color of:
- **Trust** — Essential for enterprise infrastructure
- **Technology** — The universal color of tech
- **Stability** — Infrastructure needs to feel reliable
- **Intelligence** — AI and cognitive computing
- **Depth** — Not superficial, foundational

### Why Not Purple?

Purple was intentionally avoided because:
- Overused in AI/ML branding (generic "AI purple")
- Can feel consumer-oriented rather than enterprise
- Less associated with infrastructure/reliability
- Doesn't differentiate from competitors

### Why Minimal?

The design is intentionally minimal because:
- **Infrastructure should be invisible** — It enables, doesn't distract
- **Professional over playful** — Enterprise buyers want seriousness
- **Timeless over trendy** — Avoid dating the brand
- **Confident restraint** — Doesn't need to shout

### The "Origin" Concept

**Origin** = the point where something begins.

In mathematics, the origin is (0,0) — the starting coordinate.
In workflows, the origin is where a process begins.
In AI, the origin is the prompt, the intent, the seed.

Origin Labs provides the **origin point** for AI workflows — the foundation from which everything else flows.

### The "Labs" Concept

**Labs** = experimentation, rigor, innovation.

Not "Origin Inc" or "Origin Corp" — because we're still building, testing, learning. Labs implies:
- Scientific approach
- Iteration and improvement
- Technical depth
- Research-driven

Together: **Origin Labs** = The foundation for AI innovation.

## Logo Usage

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "O" in Origin around the logo.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 24px height minimum
- Print: 10mm height minimum

### Backgrounds
- Primary: White or light backgrounds
- Acceptable: Dark backgrounds with white wordmark
- Avoid: Busy patterns, competing colors

### Don't
- Stretch or distort
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Rotate
- Change colors arbitrarily
- Use low-resolution versions

## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter
- **Headlines:** Inter Bold (700)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400)
- **Labels/Mono:** Inter Medium (500) or IBM Plex Mono

### Why Inter?
- Designed for screens
- Excellent legibility at all sizes
- Geometric but human
- Wide language support
- Free and open-source
