# Origin Labs — Brand Identity Guide v2

## Design Philosophy

**Futuristic. Professional. AI-Native.**

Origin Labs isn't just another AI company. We're building the infrastructure layer — the foundation. The brand should feel like the future, but grounded. Technical, but accessible. Sophisticated, not flashy.

---

## Logo Design

### The Concept: Origin Point

The logo centers on the letter **"O"** — the origin. It's not just a letter, it's a symbol:

- **Circle** = wholeness, the origin point, the source
- **Circuit traces** = infrastructure, connections, pathways
- **Central node** = the core, where everything connects
- **Surrounding nodes** = distributed AI, network, collaboration

The "O" becomes a **neural network node** — the starting point from which all AI workflows originate.

### Typography Integration

The words "Origin Labs" are **integrated into the logo design**:

- **"Origin"** in gradient blue (primary, bold)
- **"Labs"** in deep navy (lighter weight, creates visual hierarchy)
- The weight contrast says: "We're the origin (source, foundation) that enables labs (experimentation, innovation)"
- Gradient flows left to right: energy, motion, progress

### Logo Versions

1. **Full logo** — Symbol + wordmark integrated
2. **Wordmark only** — For narrow spaces
3. **Symbol only** — For icons, favicons, app badges

|------|-----|-----|-------|

|------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Soft Gray** | `#64748B` | 100, 116, 139 | Body text, secondary info |
| **Light Slate** | `#F1F5F9` | 241, 245, 249 | Backgrounds, cards |

### Gradient

```
Primary Gradient: #0052FF → #00A8FF → #00D4FF
```

Used for:
- Logo symbol
- "Origin" wordmark
- Accent elements
- Buttons and CTAs

### Color Psychology

**Electric Blue (#0052FF)**
- Trust + technology
- Professional, not corporate
- The color of electricity, data, future

**Cyan (#00D4FF)**
- Energy + innovation
- Digital, luminous, alive
- The color of screens, interfaces, AI

**Deep Navy (#0F172A)**
- Authority + depth
- Grounds the electric blue
- The color of space, deep tech, infrastructure

**Why not purple?**
- Purple is overused in AI (generic "AI purple")
- Can feel consumer-ish, not enterprise
- Blue/cyan feels more technical, more infrastructure

## Typography

### Primary Font: Inter

**Why Inter?**
- Designed for screens (not print-first)
- Geometric but human
- Excellent legibility at all sizes
- Wide language support
- Free and open-source

**Usage:**
- **Headlines:** Inter Bold (700)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400)
- **Labels:** Inter Medium (500) or IBM Plex Mono

### Typography Hierarchy

```
Display:     Inter 800, 48-72px
Heading 1:   Inter 700, 32-48px
Heading 2:   Inter 700, 24-32px
Body:        Inter 400, 16-18px
Caption:     Inter 500, 12-14px
Mono:        IBM Plex Mono, 12-14px
```

## Design Elements

### Circuit Pattern

The circuit traces inside the "O" can be used as a **background pattern** for:
- Website sections
- Presentation slides
- Marketing materials

Usage:
- Opacity: 5-15%
- Scale: Large, subtle
- Don't overwhelm content

### Node/Dot Pattern

The small nodes around the "O" can form **connection patterns**:
- Represents distributed AI
- Network infrastructure
- Collaboration

### Gradient Usage

Gradients should feel **electric, not rainbow**:
- Direction: Left to right (forward motion)
- Colors: Stay within blue-cyan spectrum
- Don't add unnecessary color stops
