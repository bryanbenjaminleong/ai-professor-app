# Origin Labs — Original Pitch Content (Reference)

## 1. Executive Summary

Artificial Intelligence has reached a critical inflection point. While AI models have advanced rapidly and are widely accessible, most organizations still struggle to operationalize AI in their daily workflows. AI tools exist, but the infrastructure needed to integrate AI into real work environments remains underdeveloped.

At the same time, creators and marketers increasingly rely on multiple AI tools for content generation. However, these tools operate in isolation, forcing users to manually connect different models and workflows.

Origin Labs addresses these challenges by building the infrastructure layer that enables collaboration between humans and AI systems. The company is developing two complementary platforms:

- **Crosspath** – an enterprise AI workflow engine that allows organizations to deploy AI agents capable of automating operational workflows within secure enterprise networks.
- **Mimphes** – a multi-LLM creative workflow platform that enables creators to orchestrate multiple AI models in structured pipelines for media production.

Together, these platforms establish the foundation for a new generation of systems where humans and AI collaborate seamlessly.

Origin Labs is currently raising $500,000 to $1,000,000 in seed funding to accelerate product development, expand enterprise adoption, and grow its creator ecosystem.

## 2. The Opportunity

Artificial Intelligence is moving beyond experimentation and becoming a core operational layer within organizations. However, the current generation of AI tools focuses primarily on individual capabilities rather than workflow orchestration. Most companies today face two fundamental challenges:

1. AI tools are difficult to integrate into operational systems.
2. AI workflows require significant technical expertise to build and maintain.

As a result, businesses often adopt AI in isolated use cases rather than embedding it into core operations.

At the same time, the creator economy is experiencing a surge in AI-assisted content production. Creators frequently rely on multiple AI models to generate images, videos, and digital assets. The lack of unified workflow systems forces creators to manually connect different tools.

The opportunity lies in building infrastructure that allows AI to function as part of structured workflows. Origin Labs aims to become the platform that enables this transformation.

## 3. Vision

Origin Labs believes that the future of work will involve collaboration between humans and AI systems. Organizations will increasingly operate with:

- human employees
- AI employees
- AI creative pipelines

Rather than replacing human workers, AI systems will augment human capabilities by automating repetitive tasks and accelerating creative production.

The company's long-term vision is to establish the infrastructure layer where humans and AI collaborate seamlessly across enterprise operations and creative industries.

## 4. Product Overview

Origin Labs is developing two complementary products that address different aspects of human-AI collaboration.

### Crosspath – Enterprise AI Workflow Engine

Crosspath enables organizations to deploy AI agents capable of performing operational tasks within enterprise environments. Users describe tasks using natural language prompts, and the platform automatically generates workflows composed of multiple AI agents. These agents can perform tasks such as:

- reading and processing emails
- extracting information from documents
- interacting with web applications
- updating enterprise systems
- executing browser-based automation

Crosspath also includes computer-use agents capable of interacting with graphical user interfaces. This allows automation even when APIs are unavailable.

Enterprise deployments include secure infrastructure such as private network tunnels and VPN connectivity, allowing AI workflows to operate within internal systems safely.

### Mimphes – Multi-LLM Creative Workflow Platform

Mimphes is designed for creators, marketers, and independent professionals who rely on AI for digital media production. The platform provides a visual workflow canvas that allows users to orchestrate multiple AI models within a single pipeline.

Typical workflows include: Prompt → Image Generation → Style Transfer → Editing → Final Output

Mimphes allows creators to experiment with different models and techniques while maintaining structured pipelines. The platform also supports a collaborative ecosystem where creators can share and reuse workflows. This transforms AI creation from isolated tools into a collaborative production environment.

## 5. Market Opportunity

Origin Labs operates at the intersection of two rapidly expanding markets.

### AI Automation Market

The global AI automation market is projected to exceed $100 billion in the coming years. Businesses are increasingly seeking AI solutions that can automate operational processes, reduce costs, and improve efficiency.

Crosspath targets small and medium enterprises as well as larger organizations seeking secure AI workflow automation.

### Creator Economy

The global creator economy is estimated to exceed $480 billion. AI-assisted content production is becoming a core component of digital marketing, design, and social media production.

Mimphes aims to serve creators, students, and marketing professionals who rely on AI tools to produce content.
